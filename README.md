# Urencriterium Tracker

A simple Android app that helps Dutch freelancers track whether they meet the **1225-hour criterion ("urencriterium")** required for certain tax benefits.

The app was originally built after a few freelancers asked for an easy way to keep track of their progress without needing a full bookkeeping system.

Many freelancers track their hours in spreadsheets or simple timers, but often lack a clear overview of:

- total hours worked this year
- remaining hours to reach the 1225-hour threshold
- the pace required to reach the goal before the end of the year

This app provides that overview instantly.

## Features

- Track worked hours during the year
- See remaining hours needed to reach 1225 hours
- Pace calculation based on current progress
- Export hours to Excel
- Simple interface without complex accounting features

## Target users

This app is mainly useful for:

- beginner freelancers
- freelancers without bookkeeping software
- anyone who wants a quick overview of their progress toward the 1225-hour rule
